We keep politics out of the classroom!

We teach children 'how to think', not 'what to think'? 

Our Tutors are not political activists. We don't teach CRT, and every student is treated equally.

Most parents would like to pull their children out of public school, but don't have the time to establish a curriculum or tutor their child! 

Affordable

We charge $2,000 per year. If your state offers school choice, you simply need to contact your state office, explain that you are withdrawing your child from public school, and the state will send you a school voucher. Most state vouchers exceed the total cost of attendance.

Virtual Learning & Small Classes

Our classes are conducted entirely online, and we only permit six students per class. Therefore, our maximize faculty to student ratio is 1:6. This intimate classroom environment means teachers have more time to help students, and students can better bond with their teachers and classmates. We don't leave students behind!
